Call for Papers

Submit Your Research to COLLNET 2025

Important Dates

  • Extended Abstract Submission: June 15, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: July 8, 2025
  • Full Paper Submission: July 27, 2025

We invite researchers, information professionals, and practitioners to submit their original research contributions to COLLNET 2025, the 19th International Conference on Webometrics, Informetrics & Scientometrics (WIS) and the 24th COLLNET Meeting.

This conference aims to bring together experts from different fields to explore the latest developments and innovations in scientific collaboration, research evaluation, and information science.

Submission Guidelines

Paper Format & Requirements

Extended Abstract

Submit a 500-1000 word extended abstract outlining your research, methods, results, and conclusions. Include up to 5 keywords and references. Submit in MS Word or PDF format.

Full Paper

Full papers should be between 6-10 pages, following the conference template. The papers will be peer-reviewed and accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings. Selected papers may be invited for publication in the COLLNET Journal of Scientometrics and Information Management.

Presentation

Accepted papers will be presented at the conference either in oral presentation sessions (15 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A) or poster sessions. Virtual presentations will be accommodated for those unable to attend in person.

Special Sessions

Special Session on AI in Research Metrics

This special session will focus on the role of artificial intelligence and machine learning in developing and evaluating research metrics. Papers addressing innovative approaches, challenges, and case studies are welcome.

Session Chair: Dr. Jean-Charles Lamirel, France

Proposal for Special Sessions

We welcome proposals for organizing special sessions on specific topics aligned with the conference theme. Special session proposals should include the session title, brief description, topics of interest, and a short bio of the session organizer(s).

Please submit your special session proposal to collnet2025@gmail.com with the subject line "COLLNET 2025 Special Session Proposal".

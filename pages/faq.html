General Information

COLLNET 2025 refers to the 19th International Conference on Webometrics, Informetrics & Scientometrics (WIS) & 24th COLLNET Meeting being held at the Institute of Economic Growth, Delhi, India on September 19-21, 2025. The conference brings together researchers, academics, and professionals in the fields of webometrics, informetrics, and scientometrics to share knowledge and discuss the latest developments in these fields.

COLLNET 2025 is organized by the Institute of Economic Growth, Delhi, in collaboration with COLLNET (the global interdisciplinary research network on collaboration in science and technology). The organizing committee includes distinguished academics and researchers from India and around the world. You can see the complete list of committee members on our Committee page.

COLLNET 2025 will be held from September 19-21, 2025, with a post-conference sightseeing tour scheduled for September 22, 2025, for interested participants.

Yes, COLLNET 2025 will be held in a hybrid mode, allowing both in-person and virtual participation. This format ensures that participants from around the world can attend regardless of travel restrictions or personal preferences.

You can contact the organizing committee at collnet2025@gmail.com or by phone at +91-9899110787. You can also use the contact form available on our Contact page.

Registration Information

You can register for the conference through our online registration system available on the Registration page. Complete the registration form with your personal and professional details, and follow the instructions for payment.

The registration fees for COLLNET 2025 are as follows:

  • Indian Participants: INR 5000
  • Foreign Participants: USD 250
  • Participants from SAARC Countries: USD 125

These fees include conference materials, access to all sessions, coffee breaks, lunches during the conference days, and a welcome reception.

Payment can be made through bank transfer. The bank details are available on the Registration page. Please ensure that you include your name and "COLLNET 2025" in the transfer notes for easy identification.

No, the registration fee is non-refundable. However, if you are unable to attend, you may transfer your registration to another person by notifying the organizing committee at collnet2025@gmail.com.

Yes, all presenters, whether attending in-person or virtually, must register for the conference and pay the applicable registration fee. This ensures that your paper will be included in the conference program and proceedings.

Paper Submissions

The deadline for extended abstract submission is June 15, 2025. All abstracts must be submitted by this date for consideration by the program committee.

Extended abstracts should be 500-1000 words, clearly outlining the research objectives, methodology, findings, and implications. Abstracts should be submitted in English, using MS Word format (.doc or .docx), with Times New Roman 12-point font and 1.5 line spacing. Please refer to the Call for Papers page for detailed guidelines.

Abstracts should be submitted via email to collnet2025@gmail.com with the subject line "Abstract Submission - [Your Name]". Please include your name, affiliation, contact details, and the title of your paper in the email.

Notification of abstract acceptance will be sent by July 15, 2025. If your abstract is accepted, you will be invited to submit a full paper for inclusion in the conference proceedings.

The deadline for full paper submission is August 15, 2025. Only papers from authors who have registered for the conference will be included in the final program and proceedings.

Travel & Accommodation

The conference venue, Institute of Economic Growth, is located in the University of Delhi enclave. It is approximately 25 km from Indira Gandhi International Airport. You can reach the venue by taxi, metro, or other public transportation. Detailed directions are available on the Venue & Travel page.

Limited accommodation is available in the university guest houses on a payment basis. However, participants are generally expected to arrange their own accommodation. We have provided a list of recommended hotels at different price points on the Venue & Travel page.

Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter India. We recommend checking the visa requirements for your country and applying well in advance of your travel date. India offers an e-Visa facility for nationals of many countries, which can be applied for online. For more information, please visit the official Indian e-Visa website: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/.

Yes, if you require an invitation letter for visa purposes, please send your request to collnet2025@gmail.com with the subject line "Visa Invitation Letter Request". Include your full name, passport number, and country of citizenship in the email.

The climate in Delhi in September is generally pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 15 to 30 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season begins to recede by late September, making it a comfortable time to visit. We recommend bringing light clothing, but also a light jacket for cooler evenings.

Conference Program

The detailed program will be available on the website by August 31, 2025. A tentative schedule is currently available on the Program page.

The conference will include keynote speeches, parallel sessions for paper presentations, panel discussions, and poster sessions. There will also be opportunities for networking and social events. The conference will be held in a hybrid mode, allowing both in-person and virtual participation.

The keynote speakers for COLLNET 2025 will include renowned experts in the fields of webometrics, informetrics, and scientometrics. The final list of keynote speakers will be announced closer to the conference date on the Program page.

Each oral presentation will typically be allocated 20 minutes, including 15 minutes for the presentation and 5 minutes for questions and discussion. Poster presentations will have a dedicated session where presenters can interact with attendees.

Yes, there will be a welcome reception on the first day of the conference and a conference dinner on the second day. Additionally, a post-conference sightseeing tour to some of the tourist attractions in Delhi will be arranged on September 22, 2025. Participants will be asked to bear the cost of the tour, depending on the distance, duration, and the number of delegates. Those interested may contact the organizing committee at collnet2025@gmail.com.

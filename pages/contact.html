Frequently Asked Questions

You can register for the conference by visiting our Registration page. Complete the registration form and follow the payment instructions provided.

Paper submission details are available on the Call for Papers page. You need to submit an extended abstract before the specified deadline. Full papers will be invited after the acceptance of abstracts.

To request a visa invitation letter, please email us at collnet2025@gmail.com with the subject "Visa Invitation Letter Request". Include your full name, passport number, and country of citizenship in the email.

Accommodation details are available on the Venue & Travel page. Limited rooms are available in university guest houses. Participants may also book accommodation in nearby hotels at their own expense.

About COLLNET

A Platform for Research Collaboration & Innovation

COLLNET has been a premier platform for scientometric and bibliometric research since its inception in 2000 in Berlin, Germany. Since then, COLLNET has hosted annual conferences in various locations around the world, bringing together researchers, scientists, information professionals, and policymakers.
